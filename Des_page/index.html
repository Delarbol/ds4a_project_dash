ANALYZING CRIME TRENDS IN COLOMBIA
GENERAL CONTEXT

Crime and violence are the main concerns of all citizens around the world and generates impacts on our society and exception.

Thefts, domestic violence, sexual crimes and personal injuries increased between 2010 and 2019 while murders decreased.

In 2019 in Colombia it was reported:

  • A theft every 2 minutes.
  • A domestic violence case every 5 minutes.
  • A personal injury case every 5 minutes.
  • A sexual crime every 15 minutes.
  • A homicide every 42 minutes.
THEFT, MURDERS AND ROBBERY

Theft is one of the most worrying crimes due to its exponential increase in the last three years

Men were involved in 92% of murders and in 82% of theft of cars an motorcycles

DOMESTIC VIOLENCE AND SEXUAL CRIMES

Women between 20 and 40 are more susceptible to domestic violence.

85% of sexual crimes and 82% of domestic violence cases are registered by women.

MAIN CAUSES

Illicit drug trafficking, criminal organizations and the history of violence in Colombia have a high impact in crime, especially murder rate.
